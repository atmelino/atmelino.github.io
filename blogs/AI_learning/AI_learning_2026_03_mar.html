temperature parameter
What does the temperature parameter in the language prediction do?
It varies the chances of characters with lower probabilities to be selected.
  • If the temperature is less than 1:
    characters with lower probability than the one with the highest will be made even less likely to be selected.
  • If the temperature is 1:
    no change.
  • If the temperature is greater than 1: characters with lower probability than the one with the highest will be made more likely to be
The sample() function receives the predictions of each character as a probability.
