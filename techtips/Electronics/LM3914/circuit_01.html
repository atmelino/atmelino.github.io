LM3914 circuit_01

Circuit: LED Tester

In this circuit, we will test the LM3914 and the HDSP-4850 display.

We connect REFADJ to RLO and both to ground.
We connect REFOUT to RHI
This causes the input range of the Signal at SIG to be between 0 V and 1.2 V.
Finally, we connect REFOUT to SIG.
We need a resistor between RHI and ground to control the LED brightness.
